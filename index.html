§ 05  ·  Common questions

The questions
everyone asks first.

The honest answers. If yours isn't here, send us a note. We'll get back to you within one business day, usually faster.

Still have questions?
hello@brickk.org
Do I have to manage anything myself?+

No. That's the point. Once the site is live, we handle everything. Hosting, security patches, uptime monitoring, backups. If something breaks at 2am, we fix it. You don't hear about it.

Your only job is to answer the calls the site brings in.

How fast will I see leads come in?+

Honest answer: SEO is a 60–90 day game minimum. Anyone who tells you faster is lying or running paid ads on the side and not telling you.

Realistically, expect noticeable movement in month two and meaningful change by month three, with the math working clearly in your favor by month six. SEO is a compounding effort, not a switch you flip.

What if my current site is on Wix, Squarespace, or GoDaddy?+

We migrate off it. The new site runs on a real CMS and proper hosting, which is faster, more SEO friendly, and cheaper to run month over month. You also stop being beholden to a page builder's quirks.

Can I just hire you for SEO without the rebuild?+

Sometimes. If your current site is structurally fine and we can do real work on it, yes. If it's a Wix template fighting against us at every turn, no, because we'd be lighting your money on fire. We'll tell you straight on the audit call.
